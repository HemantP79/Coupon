# Coupon
Practical Application 1 materials
This report analyzes the behavioral patterns of drivers who accepted bar-related coupons. The objective is to identify key factors influencing coupon acceptance, including demographic characteristics, environmental conditions, and behavioral habits.

Moderate users (not frequent bar-goers) are more likely to accept coupons.
Environmental conditions (temperature) influence behavior.
Demographics (age, passengers, occupation) significantly impact decisions.
High-value coupons (expensive restaurants) are less attractive.
Lifestyle factors outperform income-based factors in predicting acceptance.
Behavioral segmentation provides better insights than single-variable analysis.
